New UM Libraries Website Preview

April 24, 2018

Andrew Darby
Head, Web & Application Development

The preview version of the new University of Miami Libraries website is now available at: https://new.library.miami.edu/

We invite you to view, explore, and let us know what you think. Please keep in mind that this site is still in development. Based on your feedback, we will perform additional user testing and polish everything up over the summer in time for a launch this fall.

Why a Preview?

The purpose of the preview (or beta) launch is to give students and faculty a chance to experience the new site. The beta will allow us to test how the site performs in a realistic environment. You can look forward to more new content and features in the weeks ahead.
